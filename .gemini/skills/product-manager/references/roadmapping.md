# Roadmapping Styles and Best Practices

## Roadmap Types

### Now-Next-Later
Focuses on outcomes and priorities rather than hard dates.
- **Now:** Current sprint or quarter; high confidence.
- **Next:** Upcoming priorities; medium confidence.
- **Later:** Future explorations; low confidence.

### Timeline-Based
Traditional Gantt-style roadmaps with specific dates.
- **Best for:** Coordinating complex cross-functional launches or external commitments.
- **Risk:** High maintenance and prone to inaccuracy.

### Theme-Based
Groups work by high-level objectives (e.g., "Performance," "Onboarding").
- **Best for:** Communicating strategy to executives and stakeholders.

## Best Practices
- **Communicate the 'Why':** Every item should link back to a strategic goal.
- **Keep it Living:** Review and update at least once a month.
- **Manage Dependencies:** Clearly flag items that require cross-functional support (e.g., Marketing, Legal, DevOps).
