# Vision and Strategy Frameworks

## Product Vision
The "North Star" that describes the future state of the world once your product succeeds.
- **Formulation:** "To [ambitious goal] by [core action]."
- **Criteria:** Inspiring, clear, and long-term (3-5 years).

## Product Strategy
The plan to achieve the vision. It bridges the gap between the vision and the roadmap.
- **Key Components:**
  - **Target Audience:** Who are the primary users?
  - **Problem Space:** What is the critical pain point?
  - **Unique Value Proposition (UVP):** Why us? Why now?
  - **Business Goals:** What does success look like for the company?

## Frameworks
- **DHM Model (Gibson Biddle):** How will the product **D**elight customers, in **H**ard-to-copy, **M**argin-enhancing ways?
- **The Three Horizons (McKinsey):**
  - Horizon 1: Core business (Now).
  - Horizon 2: Emerging opportunities (Next).
  - Horizon 3: Future options (Later).
